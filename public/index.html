About Me

Hi, my name is Nicholas Ryan and I am 24 year old Software Developer specializing in Full-Stack JavaScript and Python, living in the Seattle area. I graduated from University of Oregon in 2018 with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Geology. Previously I have been a cook and manager in the food service industry, but I didn't feel fulfilled in any of those jobs. I've always loved building things, from Lego to models, and coding gives me that same satisfaction. I also love the environment coding creates, being surrounded by people who are as creative and driven as most programmers is one of the main things that drove me to the career transition. I hope you enjoy my work.
